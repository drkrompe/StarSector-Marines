multiplier gate, the two AI rocket-fire gates, and the AI projection
scan. The pattern: any time you classify a target as "hardened" /
"interesting" / "X" and the codebase has multiple call sites checking
the same property differently, ONE centralized classifier (today:
`TacticalScoring.isHardened`) prevents the AI projection / damage
pipeline mismatch.
