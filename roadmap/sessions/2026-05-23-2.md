## Design decisions

- **Per-unit vision, not per-squad** — rewards individual scout positioning.
- **visionRange > attackRange** — you spot enemies before they enter weapon range.
- **Shots NOT gated** — tracers from fog reveal enemy positions (realistic: you'd see/hear fire even if you can't see who's shooting). Intentional gameplay information leak.
- **Staggered cohorts** — 6 cohorts at 10 Hz = ~1.67 Hz refresh per contributor. Keeps per-tick cost flat regardless of contributor count. Stationary units skip entirely.
- **Ref-counted bitmap** — overlapping vision from multiple contributors handled correctly without full recompute. Death decrements; fog closes naturally.

## Bugs found and fixed in playtest

1. **Viewport edge fog gap** — fog not rendered on 4-6 tiles at viewport edges when zoomed in. Fixed: 8-cell margin on viewport bounds; scissor clips the excess.
2. **Shuttle vision missing** — player shuttles didn't contribute to fog. Fixed: ephemeral vision source system.
3. **Fighter strafe vision missing** — strafing fighters didn't reveal fog. Fixed: same ephemeral system; `FlybyOverlay.pushFighterVision()`.
4. **Double contributor registration** — `queueSpawn` serial path registered before entityId allocation; `flushPendingSpawns` registered again. Fixed: entityId gate in queueSpawn, snapshot approach in flushPendingSpawns.
