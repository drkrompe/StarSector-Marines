# S7 — Forward Operating Base (spoils-tier capstone)

> Design-forward stub. The capstone of the reach loop — the player *establishes*
> a forward LZ rather than finding one.

## Goal

A power that deploys a forward operating base into cleared territory: it becomes
a premium LZ with the deepest reinforcement reach, must be defended, and anchors
the push.

## Scope sketch

**In:**
- Deploy an FOB structure into cleared/controlled territory; it registers as a
  forward LZ for insertion powers (S6).
- Defendability — the FOB can be contested/destroyed (ties to conquest
  tug-of-war v2).

**Out:**
- (Capstone — depends on everything above.)

## Design notes

- Lore-sourced from the survey's Forward-Operating-Base family: Mora
  "buried-hull power station", the "Ilmari" mobile fabricator. These are
  **spoils-tier** acquisitions (overview § "How powers enter the roster" tier
  2), so this lands after the spoils-tier roster work exists.

## Dependencies

- S6 (drop geography), the spoils-tier roster mechanism, conquest territory
  state.
